My projects

"Zapałki" - The application is designed to be a helpful addition to the board game, allowing players to easily track and calculate their points while playing. At the end of the game, the app will determine the winner based on the accumulated points.

"Beerer" - This application is powered by a machine learning model and is designed to make it easy for users to identify different types of beer and access relevant information about them.

"GiftMe" - This application helps you keep track of the birthdays of the people who are important to you, with the added touch of personalization by allowing you to choose your own colors for each person.

"Thesis/Engineering work" - The purpose of this application is to provide a simulated environment for exploring and testing data aggregation and management techniques, utilizing both an IOT device and a central hub as part of the simulation.
