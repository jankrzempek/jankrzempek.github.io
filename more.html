CliniCheck: WBC & Cell Counter

CliniCheck is a customizable digital cell counter built for Manual WBC differentials and reticulocyte counts. It replaces traditional hardware counters with a flexible, user-friendly workspace that helps you stay focused on the microscope while keeping your results organized.

  • Customizable grids, tile sizes, spacing, and cell types to match your lab workflow
  • Eyes-free counting support with haptic feedback (and optional audio cues)
  • Attach clinical notes to sessions and securely store results for later review
  • Automatic calculations and centralized result history
  • Use your own microscopic reference images or a built-in clinical library
